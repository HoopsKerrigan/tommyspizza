Here at Tommy’s Pizzeria, we believe in one thing: Pizza. And Smiles. Family. All of the above. We strive to make you feel welcome when you walk in the door, and take pride in our food every step of the way.

Our History

Before Tommy’s was the Tommy’s you know and love, it all started with a vision, many years ago, in 2025. We here at the Berg Field Center have always wanted to share our kitchen and home cooking with the broader community, and Tommy’s has helped realize that vision. What started as a joke that became a larger and more elaborately planned joke is now realized with real people eating real pizza. And a real person reading a real website.

Our Vision

It all starts with simple, locally grown ingredients, in a fresh and sustainable farm-to-internationalshippingport-to-table chain. We are so far removed from the soil that these tomatoes grew from, the cows this cheese was milked from, and the olives this oil was pressed from, that we consider it all a wash and smile knowing that we’re part of a global chain. These local ingredients are imported at great expense and subsidized heavily by the U.S. Government to allow no cost to you. That’s what family is all about.
We appreciate your patronage, and your choice to support Tommy’s Pizzeria. Especially in the modern world of corporate-owned bullshit pizza conglomerates who corner the market like McMurdo Pizza. In a world ripe with choices, we’re glad you settled for us.
